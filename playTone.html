playTone()

Description
Plays a tone on the speaker for the specified time. The call will take the amount of time that it takes to play the tone, so if you have a duration of, say, 5000 milliseconds your Circuit Playground will spend 5 seconds playing a tone and won't be doing anything else.
NOTE: The above was based on Adafruit's documentation. However, it may not be valid. See this thread. TLDR - it's like Arduino tone().
Syntax
playTone(freq, time);
Parameters
freq [uint16_t]: The frequency of the tone in Hertz.
time [uint16_t]: The duration of the tone in milliseconds.
Returns
Nothing
